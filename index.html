    Reminders

  • Please arrive to your lash appointment with clean lashes. It can't be stressed enough how important this is for retention! If you don't have a lash cleanser, baby shampoo is perfect to use.
  • Arriving to your lash appointment with dirty lashes just means more time for cleaning and less time for lashing so keep that in mind.
  • Please try to book your refills in advance. Refills are 2-3 weeks. Anything after 3 weeks is considered a new set.

    Before the appointment

  • Remove all makeup from eye area with oil-free makeup remover.
  • Make sure lashes are squeaky clean (no mascara residue).
  • Make sure to shower before appointment. No water/steam until 24 hours.
  • Remove contact lenses.
  • 24 hour patch test required for first time clients.
    • ≈ Agree ≉ Disagree

    Aftercare

  • Avoid getting lashes near water / steam for the first 24 hours.
  • Use oil-free makeup remover.
  • Avoid getting any oil based products near the eyes. This includes tan! Always use a brush to apply tan to face.
  • Don't wear mascara.
  • Avoid touching lashes. Oils & bacteria will transfer to lashes.
  • Don't overbrush lashes.
  • Never pull or rub lashes.
  • Always get lashes removed professsionally.
